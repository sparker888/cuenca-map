# Real venue photos go here

Drop each business's images in their own folder, e.g.:

    public/listings/cafe-san-sebas/1.jpg
    public/listings/cafe-san-sebas/2.jpg

Then, in the relevant page or in `src/data/reviews.ts`, replace the
`<Illustration name="…" />` placeholder with a real `<img>`:

    <img src="/listings/cafe-san-sebas/1.jpg" alt="Café San Sebas terrace" />

Use photos the business supplies or that you shoot yourself — not images pulled
from Google Maps / Places.
